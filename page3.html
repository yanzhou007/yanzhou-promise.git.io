The stacked area graph and the grid column graph show that, governors, unlike presidents, prefer modals of volition to modals of obligation in their speeches to express commitment, especially governors of Kentucky, New Mexico, Ohio and Wisconsin.  

Among the modals of volition, will is most frequently used, especially by governor of New Mexico.
