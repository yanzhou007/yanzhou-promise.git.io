Politicians make promises,
A LOT of promises.

LANGUAGE OF PROMISES

Trump, well, probably Obama as well, and all other world leaders have made A LOT of promises. Some of the the promises are kept, many of them are not kept Tracking the status of these promises might be helpful to hold them accountable, however, we believe that the language they used in their promises also matter. 

Politicians' promises is a discourse analysis project that aims to explore the languages and topics of promises made by politicians. The current version of the project focuses on U.S. politicians, including U.S. presidents and governors. The website will be updated when the research is expended to other regions and languages in the future.
